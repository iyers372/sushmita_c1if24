ARIES

March 21st - April 19th

The Golden Ram

Phrixus and Helle were the children of Athamas, king of Boeotia, and Nephele, a cloud nymph. After Athamas was remarried to Ino, she grew jealous of the children and plotted their demise. She secretly caused a famine and then persuaded Athamas to sacrifice Phrixus to end the suffering.

However, before the sacrifice could occur, Nephele sent a miraculous Golden Ram, gifted by the god Hermes, to save her children. The ram had a brilliant fleece of pure gold and could fly. Phrixus and Helle climbed onto the ram's back, and it carried them away from danger.

In gratitude for his rescue, Phrixus sacrificed the ram to Zeus and gifted its golden fleece to Aeëtes. The ram itself was honored in the stars as the constellation Aries.

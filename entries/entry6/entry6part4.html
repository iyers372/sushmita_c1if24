Libra is also connected to Astraea, the daughter of Themis and Zeus. Astraea was the goddess of innocence and purity. During the Golden Age, she lived among humans and helped maintain peace and justice.

Themis transforms into a cruel enforcer, weighing people’s souls on her scales—not to judge them fairly, but to determine how much pain and misery they must endure to achieve "balance." For every good deed, an equal amount of suffering is meted out. The world falls into a twisted sense of equilibrium, where no act of kindness goes unpunished and no evil is left unchecked, creating a bleak, dystopian cycle of endless retribution.
